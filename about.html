About Taibu44 & Sons

Zimbabwe's Premier Consulting Firm Since 2010

Our Story

Founded in 2010 by Mr. Taibu Murehwa, Taibu44 & Sons Consulting began as a small mining advisory firm in Harare. With a vision to support Zimbabwe's economic development through expert consultation, the company quickly expanded into new sectors.

Over the years, we've grown from a team of 5 dedicated professionals to a comprehensive consulting powerhouse with over 50 experts across multiple disciplines. Our commitment to excellence and deep understanding of Zimbabwe's unique business landscape has established us as a trusted advisor to both private enterprises and government institutions.

Today, Taibu44 & Sons stands at the forefront of consulting services in Zimbabwe, offering specialized expertise in mining, gold trade, information technology, and investment sectors. Our growth reflects our unwavering dedication to our clients' success and Zimbabwe's prosperity.

Our Journey

A timeline of Taibu44 & Sons' growth and achievements

2010

Founded by Mr. Taibu Murehwa as a mining consultancy firm in Harare with a team of 5 consultants.

2012

Expanded services to include gold trade consultation, becoming one of the first specialized consultancies in this sector in Zimbabwe.

2015

Added IT consultation services to support Zimbabwe's growing digital transformation needs. Team expanded to 20 consultants.

2017

Opened a second office in Bulawayo to better serve clients throughout Zimbabwe. Began offering investment consultation services.

2020

Celebrated 10 years of service with over 300 successful projects completed. Received "Consulting Firm of the Year" award from Zimbabwe Business Excellence Awards.

2023

Expanded to 50+ consultants and formed strategic partnerships with international consulting firms to enhance service delivery to clients.

2025

Continuing to innovate and expand our service offerings to meet the evolving needs of Zimbabwe's dynamic business landscape.
